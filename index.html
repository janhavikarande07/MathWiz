Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 03/10/2024

At MathWiz, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our app, MathWiz. By using the app, you agree to the terms outlined below. If you do not agree, please refrain from using the app.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you create an account or use the app, we may collect the following personal information:

  • Email Address and Password: Collected via Firebase Authentication to securely create and manage your account.
  • Name and Birthdate: You can update your birthdate and name later in the profile section. This information is stored securely in Firestore.
  • Math Problem-Solving History: The history of math problems you’ve solved is saved and stored in Firestore for tracking your progress and improving user experience.

1.2 Images and Files

  • Camera Access: The app uses your camera to allow you to take pictures of math problems. These images are sent to a server for processing.
  • Gallery Access: Users can upload images from their device’s gallery, and these images are sent to a server for processing.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect in the following ways:
  • Account Authentication and Profile Management: Your email, name, and birthdate are stored using Firebase Authentication and Firestore to securely manage your account.
  • Equation Extraction and Solving: Images taken or uploaded are sent to a server hosted on Google Cloud Run, where the images are processed using software to extract math equations. The extracted equations are then passed to Google Generative AI for solving, and the solutions are returned to the app.
  • Problem-Solving History: We save your math-solving history to track your progress and enhance your user experience.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties, except in the following cases:
  • Firebase: Used for authentication and securely storing your personal information, such as your email, name, and birthdate.
  • Firestore: Used to store your profile information and math problem-solving history.
  • Google Cloud Run: Used to host the server where images are processed to extract math equations, and these equations are solved using Google Generative AI. No personal information is shared with Google, only the images and the extracted equations.

4. Permissions Requested by the App

To provide the core functionality of the app, we request the following permissions:
  • Camera Access: Allows users to take pictures of math problems for equation extraction and solving.
  • Gallery Access: Allows users to upload images from their device for equation extraction and solving.
  • Internet Access: Required for communication with Firebase, Firestore, and the server hosted on Google Cloud Run, which processes the images and equations.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain personal data, including your profile information and history of math problems solved, for as long as your account is active. You can request the deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at VarunBhalla25@gmail.com.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your data using Firebase's security infrastructure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. User Control Over Data

You can control your personal data in the following ways:
  • Account Deletion: You can request to delete your account and all associated data by contacting us.
  • Profile Information: You can update or delete your profile information, such as your name and birthdate, directly within the app.

8. Age Restrictions

Our app is designed for users aged 13 years and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13. If we discover that a user under the age of 13 has created an account, we will promptly delete their data. Please contact us if you believe we have collected personal information from a user under 13.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated Effective Date.
